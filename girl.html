"Stay single and build yourself. The universe will send you who deserves you."

Why we must stay single?

Have you ever known that a relationship doesn't always run smoothly? Maybe you only see it from the intimate side; you don't know what's really going on behind the scenes.


By being single, you have a lot of time to develop yourself to be better, rather than being tied to a relationship that will only bring momentary pleasure and happiness, believe me.

Being single benefits

  • You can decide what you want to do with your free time
  • You can travel anywhere, anytime you'd like
  • You can pick up and maintain any hobbies of interest
  • You won't have to make career or life-goal sacrifice for someone else
  • You can hang out with anyone
  • You may have more time to build your friendship and social circles
  • You can spare yourself from conflict and whole lot of drama
  • You could end up saving more money
  • You can wear whatever you want and decorate your place in any manner
  • You can get to know yourself and what you want a lot better
  • You can take better care of yourself
  • You can have more room to grow
  • You can become stronger, more independent, and more self-reliant
  • You can ultimately become a better partner and have a better relationship when the right person finally comes along

A few tips from me

  • Make sure you always lower your gaze towards the opposite sex, no matter when or where, with the exception of your mother, sister, or cousin.
  • Always remember this mindset: liking and loving are two different things.
  • Beauty is a relative word, not something that is certain and absolute.
  • Stop following pretty girls' Instagram accounts or something like that because that will be a trigger.
  • Thinking about girls and such can trigger a relapse of a nofap streak.
  • Stress thinking about your single life will also trigger you to smoke, believe me.

You can search for any tips and tricks for keep forward while stay single on the internet. You might have to try some new things like martial arts, sports, or going to the gym; maybe you can learn something new that you might like.
