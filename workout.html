Workout

"Tired", "lacking stamina", "always feeling lazy"? You should start wearing sports shoes and jogging for 30 minutes every day!

Why we must workout?

Imagine if you were overweight and full of fat, would people be interested in you? Would women be attracted to you? Probably not, right?


Consider starting with 25 push-ups today. If you're not used to it, start slowly with 5, then 10, 15, 20, and finally 25. With consistent effort and discipline, it will become easier over time.

Push-up Benefits

  • Burning calories
  • Protecting your shoulders and lower back from injuries
  • Improving your balance and posture
  • Enhancing flexibility
  • Boosting performance in sports and athletic activities

Apart from push-ups, you can also try sit-ups. Start with a few repetitions and then consistently increase them.

Sit-up Benefits

  • Strengthens the core
  • Improved muscle mass
  • Enhanced athletic performance
  • Better balance, stability
  • Increased flexibility
  • Improved posture
  • Reduced risk of back pain, injury
  • Strengthens diaphragm

After that, try doing squats consistently and increase the number of repetitions.

Squat Benefits

  • Strengthens the core muscles
  • Help reduce your risk of injury
  • Different squat variations can help with motivation
  • Boosts athletic performance and strength
  • Strengthens lower body muscles
  • Improved joint health
