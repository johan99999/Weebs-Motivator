Workout

"Tired", "lacking stamina", "always feeling lazy"? You should start wearing sports shoes and jogging for 30 minutes every day!

Why we must workout?

Imagine if you were overweight and full of fat, would people be interested in you? Would women be attracted to you? Probably not, right?


Consider starting with 25 push-ups today. If you're not used to it, start slowly with 5, then 10, 15, 20, and finally 25. With consistent effort and discipline, it will become easier over time.

Push-up Benefits

  • Strengthens the bones and muscles of the arms.
  • Strengthens and develops chest muscles.
  • Stamina is strengthened as the number of push-up repetitions increases.
